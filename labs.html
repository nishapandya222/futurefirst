Inspire Labs

Hands-on STEM experiences designed to inspire curiosity, creativity, and innovation in students of all ages.

About Inspire Labs

Inspire Labs are hands-on learning experiences held a few times each month for students of different age groups. These interactive labs allow students to explore STEM concepts through experiments, activities, and real-world applications. Each session is guided and monitored by experienced high school students to create a safe, engaging, and inspiring learning environment.
