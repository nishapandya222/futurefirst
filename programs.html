Our Programs

Future First creates opportunities for students to explore STEM, healthcare, and innovation through education, mentorship, research, and community involvement.

STEM Education

Providing students with opportunities to explore science, technology, engineering, and mathematics through engaging lessons, activities, and educational resources.

Healthcare Exploration

Helping students discover careers in medicine, pharmacy, and healthcare through research, discussions, and exposure to real-world healthcare topics.

Inspire Labs

A creative innovation space where students develop ideas, conduct research, and explore solutions to real-world problems through STEM.

Student-Led Classes

High school students will design and teach STEM-focused classes for middle school students, inspiring younger learners through hands-on education and mentorship.

Community Outreach

Expanding access to STEM education by connecting with students, schools, and communities to encourage curiosity and innovation.
