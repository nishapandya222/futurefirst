About Future First

Future First is a student-led organization dedicated to inspiring the next generation of innovators, scientists, and healthcare leaders through STEM education, mentorship, and community involvement.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make STEM and healthcare education more accessible for students by creating opportunities to learn, explore, and innovate. We aim to connect students with meaningful experiences that encourage curiosity, leadership, and a passion for helping others.

What We Believe

Innovation

Encouraging students to think creatively

Education

Providing students with opportunities to expand their knowledge and discover new interests.

Leadership

Empowering students to mentor others and create positive change in their communities.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every student has access to STEM and healthcare opportunities, regardless of their background. Through education, mentorship, and innovation, Future First hopes to inspire young leaders who will shape the future.
